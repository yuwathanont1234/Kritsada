1. Introduction

Luxury Authenticator ("we", "our", "the App") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and share information when you use our mobile application for luxury watch AI authentication and visual reference matching.

By using the App you consent to the data practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please do not install or use the App.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Account information: Email address used to register (no password is stored — we use magic link authentication).
  • Watch photographs: Images you capture or upload for AI analysis (front dial, caseback, side profiles).
  • Optional watch details: Custom name, purchase price, sale records you enter into your personal collection vault.
  • Subscription receipts: Anonymous receipt validation tokens from Apple App Store / Google Play (we do not see your card number).

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Diagnostic logs: App version, device platform (iOS / Android), crash reports.
  • Scan telemetry: Anonymized hashes of cohort identifiers, scan latency, model confidence scores — used to improve AI accuracy. No image content is stored in telemetry.
  • Membership tier: Stored locally on device and synced to our authentication server.

2.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not access your contacts, calendar, location, microphone, or any other system data outside the explicit Camera and Photo Library permissions you grant.
  • We do not store payment card data — all transactions are processed by Apple App Store or Google Play.
  • We do not perform facial recognition or biometric identification on any image.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • AI watch identification: Watch photos are sent to our secure inference backend (Supabase Edge Functions + Replicate DINOv3 + Google Gemini) for brand/model identification and visual similarity matching. Original photos are processed in transient memory and are not persisted on our servers beyond the duration of the API call.
  • Authentication assessment: Photos are analyzed for visual hallmarks consistent with manufacturer specifications. Results are returned to your device.
  • Service improvement: Anonymized telemetry (latency, confidence scores) helps us improve AI accuracy. We never link telemetry to your identity.
  • Subscription management: Receipts validate your access tier. We do not share receipt data with third parties.

4. Third-Party Service Providers

We use the following trusted infrastructure providers solely for app functionality:

  • Supabase (Singapore region): User authentication, encrypted database, file storage for your saved collection. Supabase Privacy Policy
  • Replicate (United States): DINOv3 image embedding inference. Images sent only for inference, not stored. Replicate Privacy Policy
  • Google Gemini API: AI watch identification + market price grounding. Google Privacy Policy
  • Apple App Store / Google Play: Subscription billing and receipt validation. Payment data never reaches our servers.

5. Data Storage & Retention

  • Your saved watch collection is stored encrypted on Supabase Singapore region (PDPA + GDPR compliant).
  • Photos uploaded for scanning are not persisted — they are processed in transient memory and discarded immediately after the embedding is computed.
  • Account data is retained until you delete your account. You may request deletion at any time via the in-app "Wipe Vault Records & History" function or by contacting us.
  • Anonymized telemetry is retained for up to 18 months for model improvement.

6. Your Rights

Under Thai PDPA, EU GDPR, and California CCPA, you have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of personal data we hold about you.
  • Rectify: Correct inaccurate data.
  • Erase ("Right to be Forgotten"): Delete your account and all associated data instantly via Settings → Wipe Vault.
  • Portability: Export your saved collection as JSON.
  • Withdraw consent: Disable cloud sync or AI processing in Settings; cancel subscription via your App Store / Google Play account.
  • Object: Disable telemetry by toggling off "Anonymous diagnostics" in Settings.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is intended for users aged 17 and above (App Store rating). We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe a child has used the App, contact us and we will delete the data immediately.

8. Security

All data transmissions use TLS 1.3 encryption. Server-side data is encrypted at rest using AES-256. We follow industry best practices including rotating credentials, RLS (Row Level Security) on database tables, and signed Edge Function invocations.

No system is 100% secure. If a data breach occurs that affects your personal data, we will notify you within 72 hours via email and in-app banner.

9. International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed in Singapore (Supabase), United States (Replicate, Google), and Thailand (your device). All transfers use Standard Contractual Clauses (SCC) for GDPR compliance and PDPA-recognized safeguards.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes via in-app banner and update the "Last Updated" date. Continued use after a change constitutes acceptance.
